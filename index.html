About Farah Design

Farah Design is a premium home gallery located in Tripoli, Lebanon. With a legacy of exceptional craftsmanship, we curate timeless collections that combine elegance, luxury, and comfort. Our designs reflect sophistication and attention to detail, tailored to meet the distinct tastes of our valued customers.

Our Story

Since 2005, Farah Design has established itself as a leader in the home design industry. From handcrafted furniture to bespoke decor pieces, our journey reflects a commitment to quality and innovation. We take pride in transforming living spaces into works of art, embodying elegance and practicality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to redefine luxury living through exceptional design and unparalleled service. At Farah Design, we aim to provide our clients with a seamless experience, offering bespoke designs that inspire beauty, comfort, and sophistication.
